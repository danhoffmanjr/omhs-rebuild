FAQ:

Q:  What is Brian’s background in Oriental Medicine?

A:  Brian graduated from the top-rated Pacific College of Oriental
Medicine in San Diego and is nationally board certified in Oriental
Medicine by the NCCAOM.  He also holds several certifications in
Oriental massage and bodywork, including Tuina.

Q:  What is Brian’s T'ai Chi background?

A:  Brian has studied T'ai Chi since 1990, studying with 2 main
instructors.  His current instructor is the incredible Chung-jen
Chang located in Bowie, MD.  Brian has studied both Yang and
Chen styles of T'ai Chi.

Q:  What is Brian’s Qigong background?

A:  Brian has studied many forms of Qigong with many instructors
since 1990.  Brian combines his knowledge of Qigong, Buddhist
meditation, Gongfu, T'ai Chi, Daoyin, and expertise in Oriental
Medicine.
