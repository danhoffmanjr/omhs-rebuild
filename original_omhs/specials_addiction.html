Sunday, Sept. 19, 2010 @ 2 pm:  Free Presentation - Stop Smoking & Other Addictions

Stop Smoking & Other Addictions:  Treating Addiction with Acupuncture & Chinese Medicine - Sunday, September 19, 2010 starting at 2 pm - FREE presentation

Discover how Acupuncture, Chinese Herbs, T'ai Chi, & Qigong can help you to kick those habits:  smoking, alcohol, food, drugs, gambling, etc.

Do you smoke or have some other addiction?  Then, you do not want to miss this FREE presentation.

How about someone you know or care about?  Get them to come along with you.

Attend and learn the basics about the Chinese Medical approach to treating addiction.

See below for details on how to get a FREE treatment during the presentation.

The following will be covered in this FREE presentation:

  • my medical background and training
  • addiction - a Western biomedical point of view
  • auricular (ear) acupuncture and its history
  • the NADA acupuncture protocol and its history
  • acupuncture needles
  • addiction treatments at OMHS
  • Chinese herbal use
  • T'ai Chi and Qigong to ease stress and gain focus

A question and answer session will follow the presentation to ensure that all of your questions are answered.  Bring your questions and bring a friend.

FREE Auricular (ear) acupuncture treatments will be given to all who pre-register.

Also, there will be chances to win FREE Qigong classes for those that pre-register (not required).

Herbal tea and light refreshments will be provided.

This presentation will be held at my Wilmington location.

As always, this presentation is FREE to the public, and there will be another presentation topic each month.  Check back often for an updated schedule.
