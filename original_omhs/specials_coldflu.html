Sunday, Oct. 17, 2010 @ 2 pm: Learn about treating and preventing colds & flus naturally with Acupuncture & Chinese Herbs.

Treating "Colds & Flus" with Acupuncture & Chinese Herbs:  Sunday, October 17, 2010 starting at 2 pm - FREE presentation

Discover how Acupuncture, Chinese herbs, T'ai chi, and Qigong can help with both the prevention of and the treatment of "colds" and "flus."  Plus, all in-stock herbal remedies will be discounted.

"Cold" and "flu" season is upon us again.  Also, the H1N1 Swine Flu is still going strong and is on everybody's mind.  Nobody wants to get sick, but sometimes we do.  Learn how to help minimize that from happening and how to cut recovery time in half if it does happen.

Attend this FREE presentation and learn how Oriental Medical modalities can help you with a variety of upper respiratory ailments.

The following will be covered in this FREE presentation:

  • my medical background and training
  • the history of assessing upper respiratory diseases in China
  • various patterns of disharmonies of upper respiratory diseases
  • diagnosis and treatment strategies
  • detailed information on common Chinese herbal remedies
  • prevention strategies
  • the many health benefits of t'ai chi & qigong

A question and answer session will follow the presentation to ensure that all of your questions are answered.

Herbal tea and light refreshments will be provided.

This presentation will be held at my Wilmington location.

Also, there will be chances to win FREE Qigong classes for those that wish to pre-register (not required).

As always, this presentation is FREE to the public, and there will be another presentation topic each month. Check back often for an updated schedule.
