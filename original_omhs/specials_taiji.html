Sunday, July 18, 2010 @ 2 pm:  Free Presentation - T'ai Chi:  An Overview & Demonstration

T'ai Chi:  An Overview & Demonstration - Sunday, Jul 18, 2010 starting at 2 pm - FREE presentation

T'ai Chi has been around for centuries and has withstood the test of time.  Originally a complex martial art, T'ai Chi has been adapted and changed over the years and is now commonly practiced for its health benefits.  Starting a regular practice of T'ai Chi now can help to ensure a healthier and longer future for you.
 
Attend this FREE presentation and learn all about T'ai Chi, its incredible range of health benefits, and what it can do for you.

The following will be covered in this FREE presentation:

  • my medical background and training
  • my T'ai Chi background and training
  • What is T'ai Chi?
  • history of T'ai Chi
  • characteristics of T'ai Chi postures and movements
  • live demonstration of T'ai Chi movements
  • T'ai Chi vs. Qigong
  • benefits of T'ai Chi

A question and answer session will follow the presentation to ensure that all of your questions are answered.

Special discounts will be offered on the day of the presentation to those wanting to sign up for classes.

Also, there will be chances to win FREE T'ai Chi or Qigong classes for those that pre-register (not required).

Herbal tea and light refreshments will be provided.

This presentation will be held at my Wilmington location.

As always, this presentation is FREE to the public, and there will be another presentation topic each month.  Check back often for an updated schedule.
