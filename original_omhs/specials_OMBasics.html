Sunday, April 18, 2010 @ 2 pm: Open House plus Free Presentation - Learn the basics about all that OMHS has to offer.

Open House + Introduction to Oriental / Chinese Healing Practices:  Sunday, April 18, 2010 starting at 2 pm - FREE presentation

This is the one that covers it all.  If you can only make it to one presentation this year, this has to be the one.

This is a special Open House event.  If you have never met me or have never been to my Wilmington, DE location, then this is the perfect opportunity to see my facility and to have all of your questions answered.
 
If you have already met me or have already been to my business, then this is a great chance to learn more about all of the wonderful and natural healing practices and services offered here at OMHS.

The following will be covered in this FREE presentation:

  • my medical background and training
  • Acupuncture
  • Chinese herbology
  • Tuina massage
  • "stop smoking" acupuncture treatments
  • T'ai Chi
  • Qigong
  • weight loss treatments
  • facial rejuvenation acupuncture

A question and answer session will follow the presentation to ensure that all of your questions are answered.

Plus, try out what an acupuncture needle feels like, for free.  There is nothing to worry about.

Herbal tea and light refreshments will be provided.

This presentation will be held at my Wilmington location.

Also, there will be chances to win FREE T'ai Chi or Qigong classes for those that wish to pre-register (not required).

As always, this presentation is FREE to the public, and there will be another presentation topic each month. Check back often for an updated schedule.
